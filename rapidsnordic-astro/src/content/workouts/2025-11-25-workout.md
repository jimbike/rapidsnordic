## Zone 3 High Intensity Training

**Date:** November 25, 2025  
**Time:** 15:30 - 17:30  
**Location:** Mt. Itasca  
**Intensity:** 9/10 (Zone 3 - High Intensity)
**Heart Rate:** >85% max

### Warm-Up (10 min)
- 10 min pole running
- a few 30 sec progressions mixed in to prep for speed

### Main Workout - Zone 3 (40 min)

#### High Intensity Intervals
- **2 x 4 minutes at 90-95% effort**
  - 3 min active recovery between
  - Maintain consistent pace
  - Do the race loop (horseshoe, swamp, camel hump, service rd, girls skip swamp)

#### Speed Development
- **6 x 30 seconds MAX effort**
  - 90 seconds full recovery
  - run this along the biathlon range, keep it flat

### Strength Integration (15 min)
- Power-focused exercises:
  - Jump squats
  - Pushups
  - Planks 

### Cool Down (15 min)
- 10 min very easy run
- Yoga and streching

### Key Points
- This is a breakthrough session
- Quality over quantity
- Full effort required
- Need 48 hours recovery after
