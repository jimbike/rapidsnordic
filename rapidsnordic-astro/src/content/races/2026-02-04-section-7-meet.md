## Race Details

The Section 7 Meet is our championship qualifying race for the State meet. This is the culmination of our season's training and preparation.

### Bus Schedule
- **Departure:** 6:30 AM from GRHS Parking Lot
- **Return:** Approximately 5:00 PM to GRHS

### Schedule
- **Arrival Time:** 8:00 AM
- **Race Start:** 10:00 AM
- **Format:** Classic Sprint and Pursuit

### What to Bring
- Both Classic and Skate skis (waxed at Feb 3 wax session)
- Racing suit or team uniform
- Warm-ups and extra layers
- Water, snacks, and lunch
- Team tent supplies
- Extra equipment for sprints

### Race Format
- **Morning:** Classic Sprint qualifying and heats
- **Afternoon:** Pursuit races
- **Qualification:** Top finishers advance to State

### Course Information
Giants Ridge championship courses will be in prime condition for this important meet. Athletes should draw on their experience from earlier races here.
