## Race Details

The Mesabi East Invite is one of the season's premier events, held at the excellent Giants Ridge facility. This race features both skate and classic competitions.

### Bus Schedule
- **Departure:** 7:30 AM from GRHS Parking Lot
- **Return:** Approximately 4:00 PM to GRHS

### Schedule
- **Arrival Time:** 9:00 AM
- **Race Start:** 10:00 AM
- **Format:** Skate and Classic events

### What to Bring
- Skate skis (waxed at Jan 9 wax session)
- Classic skis if participating in both events
- Racing suit or team uniform
- Warm-ups and extra layers
- Water, snacks, and lunch
- Hand warmers

### Course Information
Giants Ridge offers championship-level trails with excellent grooming. The venue has hosted numerous state and regional championships. Athletes should be prepared for challenging climbs and fast descents.

### Venue Facilities
- Indoor warming area available
- Concessions on site
- Excellent spectator viewing areas
