## Race Details

The Cook County Invite takes us to the beautiful Pincushion Mountain trail system for a classic technique race.

### Bus Schedule
- **Departure:** 6:00 AM from GRHS Parking Lot
- **Return:** Approximately 6:00 PM to GRHS

### Schedule
- **Arrival Time:** 9:00 AM
- **Race Start:** 11:00 AM

- Bib Pick-up: in Chalet from 8-10:00 AM
- Coaches Meeting: 10:30 AM in Chalet

- 11:00- Girls Varsity 5.4K Classic Interval Start (two racers every 30 seconds)
- 11:20- Boys Varsity 5.4K Classic Interval Start (two racers every 30 seconds)
- 11:40- Girls Junior Varsity 5.4K Classic Interval Start (two racers every 30 seconds)
- 12:00- Boys Junior Varsity 5.4K Classic Interval Start (two racers every 30 seconds)
- 12:20- Boys JH 3.1K Classic Interval Start (two racers every 30 seconds)
- 12:40- Girls JH 3.1K Classic Interval Start (two racers every 30 seconds)
- *1:20- Mixed Gender Four Person Classic Relay Race Mass Start (4 laps of 1.4K)
*Fun Event. This will consist of two girls and two boys- Boy/Girl/Boy/Girl. Each school can enter 1 team (racers CAN compete in the other events and still do the relay). Each school will have a lane to seat their teams. The course will be a 1.4K with each racer doing ONE lap and an exchange/finish zone in the stadium. The timing clock will be left running, but the race will not be “officially” timed. Coaches will be asked to help.

### What to Bring
- Classic skis (waxed at Dec 19 wax session)
- Racing suit or team uniform
- Warm-ups and extra layers
- Water and snacks
- Extra kick wax for touch-ups

### Course Information
Pincushion offers scenic trails with varied terrain. Athletes should be prepared for both gradual climbs and technical sections.
