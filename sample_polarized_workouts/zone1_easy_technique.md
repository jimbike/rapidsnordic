## Easy Technique Development

**Date:** December 03, 2025  
**Time:** 15:30 - 17:30  
**Location:** Mt. Itasca  
**Intensity:** 4/10 (Zone 1 - Easy)
**Heart Rate Target:** 60-70% max HR

### Purpose
Build aerobic base while refining technique. This session emphasizes quality movement patterns at low intensity, allowing athletes to focus on form without fatigue interference.

### Warm-Up (20 min)
- 10 min very easy skiing
- 5 min dynamic stretching and mobility
- 5 min balance drills on skis

### Main Workout (70 min)
#### Technique Circuit (Repeat 2x)
- 10 min Classic technique focus
  - Emphasis on weight transfer
  - Focus on glide phase
  - Maintain conversational pace
- 5 min easy ski between techniques
- 10 min Skate technique focus
  - V2 rhythm work
  - Edge control
  - Stay relaxed and smooth
- 5 min easy recovery ski

#### Drill Work (20 min)
- 10 x 1 min technique-specific drills
  - No-pole skiing
  - Single-ski glide
  - Tempo changes
- Full recovery between each
- Focus on form, not speed

### Cool Down (20 min)
- 15 min very easy ski
- 5 min static stretching

### Key Points
- **Stay in Zone 1**: Should feel easy throughout
- **Focus on form**: This is skill development, not fitness
- **Breathe naturally**: Should be able to maintain conversation
- **Quality over quantity**: Better to ski well slowly than poorly fast

### Notes
- If you feel the urge to go harder, resist it
- This session builds the aerobic engine that powers everything else
- Perfect technique at low speed transfers to high speed

### Equipment Needed
- Both Classic and Skate skis if available
- Heart rate monitor recommended
- Water bottle
